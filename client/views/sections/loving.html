Loving

My heart is always been at the Back-end side. I´ve always been amazed about design patterns and the art of making growing software modular and scalable. I never had any special feeling about the UI side.

But then, a few years ago, world changed. With all kind of screen sizes moving arround, the world has reinvented itself. Connected through a cloud that has incredibly grown in capabilities. So many kind of devices, so many new concepts to be created.
Reaching a point that the art of UX has been inevitably stablished and web development has become something with tremendous potential. With plenty of new frameworks that provide us a way to reach this potential.

We all have had very nice ideas. And enjoy searching for them. But sooner or later someone learns that there is no idea without its execution.
So I decided I´d love to be able to execute anyting could came to my mind, to learn the Full-Stack. And I am using MEAN:

  • I enjoy with AngularJS especially now that I´ve gone deep into the framework, now that I completely understand the pourpose of its components. When to use what and when not to. And what´s even more important: to comprehend its limitations.
  • I admire their decision for such a radical change at AngularJS 2, getting rid of so many basic components and concepts of the first framework. I think it´s what was needed and I am looking forward to give it a shot.
  • I like pure REST APIs architecture for CRUD apps and I enjoy developing them with Express.
  • I am pleased by storing data rich structures in MongoDB without actually caring about what and how those structures are.
  • And although I´ll always love OOP, I have to admit that it is kind of cool developing with NodeJS a Back-end that works concurrently and in the same language as my Front-end.

And I don´t remember my self to enjoy creating software as I do it right now, and that´s why I am sure I´m a grate asset.
