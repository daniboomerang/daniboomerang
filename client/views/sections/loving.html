Loving

My heart is always been at the back-end side. I´ve always been amazed about design patterns and the art of making growing software modular and scalable. I never had any special feeling about the UI side.

And then, a few years ago, a huge technological revolution changed things, right?.
The world has reinvented itself, with all kind of screen sizes moving arround, and all connected by a cloud that has inclredibly grown in capabilities.
So many kind of devices, so many new concepts to be created.
Reaching a point that the art of UX has been stablished and web development has become something with tremendous potential and plenty of new frameworks that provide us a way to reach this potential.

We all have had really cool ideas. And enjoy looking for them. But sooner or later everyone learns that there is no idea without its execution. So a couple of years ago I decided I´d love to be able to execute anyting that came to my mind. And so I decided to became Full-Stack Web developer. And this is what I enjoy today and I am focused doing it with MEAN.

And hey, everything changes and I am sure there are plenty of projects that have nothing to do with what I do now and that I´d love to participate in, and so I am all ears.But this is what I love doing today:

  • I enjoy AngularJS especially now that I´ve gone deep into the framework, now that I understand the pourpose its components, when to use what and when not to. And much more important: to get its limitations.
  • I admire their decision to such a radical change for AngularJS 2, getting rid of so many basic components and concepts of the first framework. I think it´s what was needed and I am looking forward to give it a shot
  • I like REST APIs now I am happy developing pure with Express.
  • I am pleased by storing data rich structures in MongoDB without actually caring about what and how this structures are.
  • And although I´ll always love OOP, I have to admit that it is kind of cool developing with NodeJS a back-end that works concurrenty and in the same programming language as my Front-end.

I strongly believe productivity arises with the ability to find what you freely decide to do and simply love to do. And I don´t remember my self enjoying creating software as I do it right now, and that´s why I am sure I´m a good asset.
