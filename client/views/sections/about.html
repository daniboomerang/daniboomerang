About

Since I finished university I always wanted to work in a big company and big and complex products.
I wanted the complex, the tangled, the tricky and I was exited about the opportunity to learn how to deal with it. Where the only way to modelize all that was a tremendous mastery of dessign patterns and developing technical skills.
And although I haven´t forgotten about all that and I recognize and admire any work like this, now I see things quite differently.

There is nothing in this world I admire more than the ability to change, to adapt, to beat the unsecurity of the unknown, to be over the comfort of the tradicion and be aware of it.
I am delighted by the art of recongnition what is over and must be reinvented and renewed. Because I strongly believe that anyone´s happiness or any company´s survival depends in one way or another, at the very end on this factor.
And that´s why I just get impressed by people´s or companies dynamism, if agility or creativity are essential adjectives that describe their culture, philosophy or methodology.
I believe creativity is not a particullar skill that can be improved, something that you learn or not. Creativity ain´t knowledge nor memory. Creativity is the new, and only the thought forgetting its assumptions and old traditions reaches creativity.

I am looking for a company that has understood that their old methods are not the only methods and it has the courage to change to something else. When necessary, if necessary.
I am looking for a job where responsability, objectives and communication, are the only things that matter. I´ve seen the wonderful thing about working where and when I want. I´ve never reached such productivty in my life.
There is absolutely nothing wrong about going to an office but I believe most of the people has accepted this as the way life is, and most of the companies have acquire an specfic way of thinking, a specific methodology and assumed that´s the only way of working.

But it is not.
It is crystal clear for me that productivity has nothing to do with the numbers of hours sat in a stipulated place, the number of incidences solved and the number of commited lines of code, but rather with doing what you freely decide to do. Only when there is a free commitment with what you love then there is no complain of any type. And only when complaint doesn´t arise, there is happiness

I believe today there are plenty of assumptions about how, when and where we must work.
I marvel at people who has understood the implications of the assumption, the danger of the static nature of that.
I believe that we are lucky, and software can be done in many different ways, of course none of them absolutely perfect, each one with its own limitations.

I like to decide working when is the moment to work, which doesn´t neccesary have to be at 8:00AM everyday.
It could be at anytime. I could be sometimes splited in improvised and asymetric periods. It could be in the middle of the night, at lunch or breakfast time. It could be for 200 hours in a row or it could need a 24 hours break with non thinking at all. I believe there is a huge potetial if you don´t put boundaries at the mind to do what she loves when she loves.
I like to work anywhere I can find a minimum concentration, which doesn´t necessary have to be at an static office.
It could be in a plain heading to anywhere, at a cafe looking at the sunset, in a train, at my home or the library. I believe there is a huge potetial if you don´t put boundaries at the mind to do what she loves where she loves.

Creativiy arises when thought pulls apart the stipulated, its traditions, its asumptions, its conditioning

I am specially interested on wroking with people that share´s anything exposed above.
I know in the last few years many companies have founded new approaches, new perspectives in their way of working and I think I could be a great asset to them.
